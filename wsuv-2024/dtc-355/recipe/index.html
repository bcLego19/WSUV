Best Pizza Dough

The recipe indicates you can use it right away and make your own pizza. We let it sit and rise about 30 minutes before making our pizzas. Either way this recipe worked out very well.

  • TIME: 15 minutes
  • TOOLS: A round pizza pan with tiny holes in it
  • DIET: Gluten
  • SERVING: 6
  • 3 cups flour
  • 2 1/2 tsp bulk yeast

    (Bulk yeast can be substituted with 1 package dry yeast)

  • 2 tbsp oil (I use canola)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp white sugar
  • 1 cup warm water (110 degrees)
  • 1. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl
  • 2. Cover and let sit and rise for 30 minutes
  • 3. Spread pizza dough on round pizza pan
  • 4. Add tomato sauce, cheese, mushrooms, sausage, ham, pineapple, olives, green or red peppers, chicken, onion, artichokes, etc.
  • 5. Bake about 425 degrees for 8-15 minutes. Watch closely.
